Takayuki Nagamine



I am a final year PhD candidate at Lancaster University, UK. I use ultrasound tongue imaging to investigate articulation in second language speech, particularly English liquids produced by L1 Japanese speakers.

My PhD research is funded by Japan Student Services Organisation (JASSO) and The Murata Science Foundation.
